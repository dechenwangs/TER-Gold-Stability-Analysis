About the Research

Purpose, Background
& Objectives

Evaluating TER's stability and trustworthiness through five years of market data (2020–2025) and an original investor survey — focused on Bhutan's emerging digital financial ecosystem.

TER — from the Dzongkha word གཏེརི meaning “Treasure” — is the world's first sovereign-backed physical gold-backed digital token. Launched on Bhutan's National Day, December 17, 2025, issued by Gelephu Mindfulness City, running on Solana, custodied by DK Bank.

“TER marks a foundational step toward a values-driven digital economy rooted in real assets and sovereign trust — demonstrating how a crypto-friendly city can welcome responsible innovation while staying rooted in Bhutan's values.”

As a personal TER investor, this research combines academic rigour with lived investment experience — examining whether a gold-backed token can truly offer greater stability than volatile cryptocurrencies.

Conclusion

Key Insights &
Implications

TER's gold backing delivers real stability. The corrected survey data shows distrust of digital assets is the biggest barrier — trust-building is as important as education.

“Rooted in Bhutan's spirit of trust, integrity, and responsible finance, TER embodies the nation's commitment to inclusive, sustainable innovation — bridging the ancient treasure of gold with the digital economy of tomorrow.”

Market data confirms TER delivers on its stability promise — volatility comparable to gold (~13%) and dramatically lower than Bitcoin (~72%) or Ethereum (~82%). This validates the core research hypothesis.

The corrected hesitation treemap reveals: 33.33% do not trust digital assets at all. Another 22.22% prefer “traditional options like gold” — yet TER IS backed by gold, making them a natural audience. Trust is a bigger gap than education.

For GMC, TER is proof that Bhutan's ancient value of treasuring what is precious (གཏེརི) can thrive in the age of blockchain and digital sovereignty.

01

Build Digital Asset Trust

Address the 33.33% who “don't trust digital assets” — through audits, reserve reporting, and RMA regulatory clarity.

02

Reframe TER as Traditional Gold

The 22.22% preferring “traditional options like gold” ARE TER's target audience. TER is gold — messaging must make this explicit.

03

Address Volatility Perception

The 22.22% who find digital assets “too risky” need to see TER's ~13% vs Bitcoin's ~72% — the data tells a powerful story.

04

Education through DK Bank

The 11.11% who “don't understand” are the most fixable barrier — targeted demos and explainers through DK Bank channels.

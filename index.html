Project Context

Why This
Internship Project?

On Bhutan’s 118th National Day, December 17, 2025, something quietly historic happened: TER — a sovereign, gold-backed digital token — entered circulation. I invested. Not out of speculation, but out of belief in what Bhutan was building.

But belief and understanding are not the same thing. Even as an investor, I found myself uncertain about the mechanics, the stability, the trust I was placing in a system I couldn’t fully explain. That honest gap became the question this internship project set out to answer — with data.

"Bhutan sits at a unique crossroads — a country that measures success by Gross National Happiness, now building the world's first sovereign gold-backed digital economy. This project captures that moment through data."

Internship Project Objectives
01

Market Stability Analysis

Comparing Gold, Bitcoin and Ethereum annualised volatility using 5 years of historical price data (2020–2025). TER's volatility is projected from gold's data as TER is fully gold-backed. Source: Investing.com · The Block · World Gold Council

02

Investor Trust Survey

Original quantitative survey of 125 respondents to measure TER awareness, trust levels, and investment intentions in Bhutan.

03

Adoption Barriers

Identify what holds Bhutanese investors back from digital assets — and what would change their mind about TER.

04

Strategic Recommendations

Data-backed recommendations for improving TER's adoption, trust, and positioning as a gold-backed digital instrument.

05
Prototype

TER Pay — Everyday Payment Platform

A working prototype that allows users to make everyday payments using TER. TER Pay is designed to bridge the gap between investment and usability — letting people experience TER's stability through real transactions. By paying with TER in daily life, users build practical understanding and trust that data alone cannot always achieve. The prototype covers the full payment flow: QR scanning, gold-to-TER conversion, confirmation, merchant dashboard, and auto-save features.

Why This Internship Project

A Story That Begins with a Personal Investment

01
The Investment

I invested in TER — and realised I didn't fully understand it.

When TER launched on Bhutan's 118th National Day in December 2025, I was drawn in by the idea of supporting Bhutan's digital economy. I invested — mainly to be part of something I believed in. But even after investing, I found myself unsure about exactly how TER worked, what backed it, and whether my investment was truly stable. That uncertainty stayed with me.

02
The Realisation

If I was confused, others probably were too.

The more I reflected on my own confusion, the more I suspected I wasn't alone. There are likely many others like me — people who are genuinely interested in TER and willing to support it, but still feel unsure or unclear about how the system works. That gap between curiosity and understanding felt like exactly the kind of problem worth investigating through data.

03
The Question

Can data bridge the trust gap?

My internship at Bhutan Data Scientists Pvt. Ltd. gave me the perfect platform to turn that personal question into a structured internship project. I wanted to understand — through real data — whether TER's gold-backing truly delivers the stability it promises, and what it would take for investors like me to feel genuinely confident in it.

04
The Internship Project

Five years of data, 125 voices, one clear story.

This internship project draws on five years of gold, Bitcoin, and Ethereum price data, alongside an original survey of 125 Bhutanese respondents. Together, they tell a compelling story: TER's gold backing provides real, measurable stability — and with the right communication and education, it can earn the trust of investors who are, right now, just waiting to understand it better.

To bring this insight to life, the project also includes TER Pay — a prototype payment platform that allows users to make everyday transactions using TER. Rather than treating TER purely as an investment asset, TER Pay demonstrates its potential as a usable currency in daily life. By experiencing TER through real payments, users can better understand how it works, witness its stability firsthand, and build the kind of genuine confidence that data alone cannot always convey.

About the Internship Project

Purpose, Background
& Objectives

Evaluating TER's stability and trustworthiness through five years of market data (2020–2025) and an original investor survey — focused on Bhutan's emerging digital financial ecosystem.

TER — from the Dzongkha word གཏེརི meaning "Treasure" — is the world's first sovereign-backed physical gold-backed digital token. Launched on Bhutan's National Day, December 17, 2025, issued by Gelephu Mindfulness City, running on Solana, custodied by DK Bank.

"TER marks a foundational step toward a values-driven digital economy rooted in real assets and sovereign trust — demonstrating how a crypto-friendly city can welcome responsible innovation while staying rooted in Bhutan's values."

As a personal TER investor, this internship project combines academic rigour with lived investment experience — examining whether a gold-backed token can truly offer greater stability than volatile cryptocurrencies.

The internship project draws on five years of historical price data (2020–2025) for gold, Bitcoin, and Ethereum, alongside an original quantitative survey of TER investors and potential adopters in Bhutan's growing digital economy.

Does gold offer greater stability than Bitcoin and Ethereum? And if so, can TER — being fully gold-backed — be projected to offer the same? Tested using five years of historical price data (2020–2025). Source: Investing.com · The Block

Market Stability Analysis

Stability, Volatility
& Asset Behaviour

A time-series comparison of Gold, Bitcoin, and Ethereum prices from 2020 to 2025. Since TER is fully backed by physical gold (Source: ter.bt), gold's historical volatility serves as the closest available benchmark for TER's projected price behaviour.

Chart 1 · Price Trend Comparison 2020–2025
Gold vs Bitcoin vs Ethereum — Normalised Price Movement
Index base 100 = Jan 2020 · Shows relative price movement, not absolute price · Data: Investing.com, The Block, World Gold Council
What this chart tells us · from this internship project

Bitcoin shows the highest volatility, with sharp rises in 2021 and 2024–2025, and a major correction in 2022. Ethereum follows a similar pattern at a smaller scale. Gold remains relatively stable throughout, with gradual upward movement and far fewer sharp fluctuations. The 2022 crypto downturn is clearly visible — a broad market-wide correction that did not significantly affect gold. The 2023 recovery phase marks where crypto markets began rebounding after the crash. Gold demonstrates long-term stability, while cryptocurrencies provide higher growth potential with significantly higher risk.

Chart 2 · Annual Absolute Volatility 2020–2025
Bitcoin vs Ethereum vs Gold — Volatility Comparison View
Lower value = more price stability · Gold consistently the lowest · Data: The Block, Investing.com, World Gold Council
What this chart tells us · from this internship project

Ethereum has the highest volatility overall, peaking sharply in 2021. Bitcoin follows a similar pattern, also peaking in 2021 before both drop sharply in 2023 and partially recover in 2024–2025. Gold maintains consistently low, near-flat volatility across all six years — hugging the bottom of the chart throughout. Crypto assets experience large volatility swings year-to-year, while gold remains stable — supporting the projection that TER, being fully gold-pegged, would inherit similar low-volatility characteristics. Note: TER launched Dec 2025; empirical TER volatility data is not yet available.

Market data shows gold's annualised volatility is dramatically lower than Bitcoin or Ethereum. Since TER is fully backed by physical gold (ter.bt), it is projected to inherit this same low-volatility profile — though empirical TER data remains limited given its December 2025 launch. Cryptocurrencies outperform during expansion phases but suffer large corrections during downturns. Gold acts as a stabilising asset across market cycles — and TER is built to carry that same characteristic into the digital economy.

The survey data reveals that 66.4% of respondents do not yet invest in financial assets — a significant untapped audience if awareness grows. Meanwhile, 78 respondents showed willingness to invest in gold, directly validating TER's gold-backed approach. Trust, perceived risk, and preference for traditional financial systems are the biggest adoption barriers.

Most encouragingly, over 80% expressed positive intention to hold TER, and 88.24% prefer gold-backed digital assets for long-term value — validating the core internship project hypothesis that combining gold's stability with digital accessibility is TER's key competitive advantage.

Education through DK Bank

The 11.11% who "don't understand" are the most fixable barrier — targeted demos and explainers through DK Bank's existing channels can close this gap.

These screens represent the DK Bank TER Wallet prototype v5 Clean, designed around the user story of Karma and Deki. Core design principles: (1) balance hidden by default to protect financial privacy, (2) gold backing shown prominently at the trust moment (Screen 3), (3) auto-save is merchant-only and never visible to customers, (4) payment confirmation uses PIN — not biometric — to be device-agnostic, (5) both sides see only what is relevant to them, (6) the new auto-save rate modal (Screen 5b) gives merchants full control over their savings rate with six preset options and a custom input.

Key Distinction from DK Bank's Existing Approach

The optional auto-save in gold-backed TER (not fiat) is what separates this system. When the merchant enables it, every payment automatically grows their savings in TER — preserving gold-backed value. This is the incentive that motivates a busy merchant to do the work of setting up a merchant wallet.

Existing (3 steps)

Steps 1, 8, and 9 already exist in the TER app — Solana blockchain broadcast and TX ID recording are core infrastructure already built.

Partial (2 steps)

Gold oracle query and simultaneous confirmation exist but need upgrading — the oracle needs to be hooked into the payment UI, and simultaneous confirmation needs merchant-side testing.

New (7 steps)

QR scanner, conversion UI, confirmation screen with balance-hiding, user authorisation, merchant dashboard, auto-save feature — these are the new builds that complete the TER-Pay experience.

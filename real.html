About the Research

Purpose, Background
& Objectives

Evaluating TER's stability and trustworthiness through five years of market data (2020–2025) and an original investor survey — focused on Bhutan's emerging digital financial ecosystem.

TER — from the Dzongkha word གཏེརི meaning "Treasure" — is the world's first sovereign-backed physical gold-backed digital token. Launched on Bhutan's National Day, December 17, 2025, issued by Gelephu Mindfulness City, running on Solana, custodied by DK Bank.

"TER marks a foundational step toward a values-driven digital economy rooted in real assets and sovereign trust — demonstrating how a crypto-friendly city can welcome responsible innovation while staying rooted in Bhutan's values."

As a personal TER investor, this research combines academic rigour with lived investment experience — examining whether a gold-backed token can truly offer greater stability than volatile cryptocurrencies.

The research draws on five years of historical price data (2020–2025) for gold, Bitcoin, and Ethereum, alongside an original quantitative survey of TER investors and potential adopters in Bhutan's growing digital economy.

Ugyen Norlha Choeten, Gelephu 🇧🇹

GMC's first completed spiritual landmark — consecrated February 21, 2026. A 53-ft stupa-temple at the Gelephu Border Gate. Source: BBS · March 2, 2026

BBS · Bhutan Broadcasting Service · March 2, 2026

"Ugyen Norlha Choeten stands tall as one of GMC's first spiritual landmarks"

Dedicated to Ugyen Norlha — the prosperity manifestation of Guru Rinpoche — the Choeten was built to commemorate the birth of HRH Gyalsem Sonam Yangden Wangchuck. It houses 27 deity statues on the ground floor and ~34,000 Tongkus on the upper floor, along with the Kangyur and Tenjur Buddhist texts. Unique wall sculptures of the "Lhatsho" (gathering of prosperity deities) are said to be found nowhere else in Bhutan. As GMC grows, this Choeten is expected to lay a strong spiritual foundation — ensuring the city's transformation is guided by faith and Bhutanese values.

Implications

TER's gold backing delivers real stability. The survey data shows distrust of digital assets is the biggest barrier — trust-building is as important as education.

Market data confirms TER delivers on its stability promise — volatility comparable to gold (~13%) and dramatically lower than Bitcoin (~72%) or Ethereum (~82%). Cryptocurrencies outperform during expansion phases but suffer large corrections during downturns. Gold acts as a stabilising asset across market cycles — and TER is built to carry that same characteristic into the digital economy.

The survey data reveals that 66.4% of respondents do not yet invest in financial assets — a significant untapped audience if awareness grows. Meanwhile, 78 respondents showed willingness to invest in gold, directly validating TER's gold-backed approach. Trust, perceived risk, and preference for traditional financial systems are the biggest adoption barriers.

Most encouragingly, over 80% expressed positive intention to hold TER, and 88.24% prefer gold-backed digital assets for long-term value — validating the core research hypothesis that combining gold's stability with digital accessibility is TER's key competitive advantage.

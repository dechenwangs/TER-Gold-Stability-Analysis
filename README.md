TER-Gold-Stability-Analysis

Project Overview

This project evaluates the stability and trustworthiness of TER, a gold-backed digital asset, by analyzing historical gold price trends alongside major cryptocurrencies (Bitcoin and Ethereum) and integrating survey data on user awareness, trust, and adoption willingness. 

Central Question: 

Can a gold-backed digital asset like TER offer greater stability and trust compared to traditional cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum?

The study also focuses on TER’s potential adoption in Bhutan, particularly within Gelephu Mindfulness City (GMC), where it could serve as a foundation for a new digital financial ecosystem.

Purpose & Significance

This project is intended for:  

Potential investors and users seeking stable, asset-backed digital currencies.  
Policymakers and regulators shaping Bhutan’s digital economy.  
Analysts and researchers studying digital assets and adoption patterns.  


Why it matters: 

Provides insights into how TER’s gold backing affects its stability and adoption potential.  
Combines market analysis and human perception data for a comprehensive understanding.  
Personal Investor Perspective: I have personally invested in TER, which motivates me to analyze its stability and adoption potential beyond academic curiosity. By connecting my investor experience with data analytics skills, I can explore TER’s role in Bhutan’s emerging digital economy.

Methodology

- Cleaned historical price data and calculated percentage changes for comparison.

Visualization Techniques:  

- Line charts for historical price trends  
- Timeline comparisons for volatility  
- Bar charts and pie charts for survey responses  
- Integrated dashboards linking market data and human perception

Key Insights

1. Stability Comparison
   
- TER has potential to offer greater stability and trust because it is backed by gold, a historically stable asset.
  
  Market Analysis: 
  - Gold shows gradual, steady price movements.  
  - Bitcoin and Ethereum exhibit sharp fluctuations and high volatility.  
- Implication: TER may serve as a predictable, stable financial instrument in a digital ecosystem.

2. Survey-Based Perceptions
   
- Awareness: 42.02% of respondents reported knowing a little about TER.
  
Trust Levels: 

  - 58.82% have moderate trust 
  - 26.47% have complete trust
    
Financial Engagement:
  
  - 58.33% invest at a moderate level  
  - 27.08% invest completely
    
Hesitation Factors:
  
  - Limited understanding of digital assets  
  - Concerns about volatility  
  - Preference for traditional financial instruments  

Implication: While interest exists, adoption depends on education, awareness, and clear communication about TER’s gold backing and functionality.

3. Recommendations for Adoption
   
1. Education & Awareness Initiatives – Help users understand TER and its stability.  
2. Communicate Security Measures – Explain risk management and platform safeguards.  
3. Address Common Concerns – Provide information to counter misconceptions about digital assets.  
4. Promote Transparency – Share clear details about TER’s gold backing, functioning, and stability.  

Outcome: By improving awareness and trust, TER can become a widely accepted digital asset, supporting the financial ecosystem in Gelephu Mindfulness City.
